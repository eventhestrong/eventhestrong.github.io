Crude Futures Zine, a magazine produced with New Models

Crude Futures is a magazine created by writers living in societies that feel increasingly in a state of precarious disrepair—which is to say, all of them.

Speculative vignettes, apocalyptic narratives, diagrammatic catastrophes, photographic treatments, frameworks for worldbuilding, cerebral germs by: Beau-Caprice Vetch, Richard Hames, Mina Miller, Jack Tarpey, Jake Colvin, phm, Matīss Groskaufmanis, Jon Benjamin Talleräs, Cat MacGregor, T.M. Wilson. Design & layout: Marcelino Llano.
